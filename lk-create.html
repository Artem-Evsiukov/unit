Important!

Creating a token is simple, but you should carefully consider your total supply and to a lesser extend your symbol (ticker) as neither of these can be changed after creation.

It is also the case that there cannot be two of the same symbols on Unit Network and in preparation for interoperatble networks where your token could be used outside this platform, you might consider not to choose existing tickers e.g. BTC, ETH, DOT etc

Need Help?

Depending on your specific project or token use case deciding on your total supply could be very straight forward i.e “I need to raise $10,000 to release my new album, so I’ve created a total supply of 10,000 tokens”.

However for established business who have more complex evaluations to make we’d strongly recommend you join our Unit Ventures program so you can get assistance from an experienced team before diving in.
