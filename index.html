ALL THE TOOLS TO BUILD YOUR OWN TOKEN ECONOMY

Token Creator: Free and simple token creation available to anyone in the world

Events: Create events to support, grow and galvanise your community and token holders

Wallet: Allows deposits and free withdrawal of all digital assets as well as transfer, staking / unstaking

News: Publish updates, announcements and news articles to keep your community informed

Exchange: Buy, sell or stake your tokens within the platform using a dencentralised, AMM Protocol

Contests: Create contests and offer rewards to your most dedicated token holders

Token Treasury: Used to establish token value + floor price and improve token transparency

Token Sale: Carefully manage your token sale with control of sale price, stages and team rewards

Blockchain Explorer: Showing a transparent and permanent history of all transactions.
